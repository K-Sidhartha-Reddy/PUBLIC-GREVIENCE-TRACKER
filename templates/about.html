About the Portal

Transforming governance through digital innovation and citizen engagement

Our Mission

To provide a transparent, efficient, and accessible digital platform that bridges the gap between citizens and government, ensuring timely resolution of grievances and promoting participatory governance.

Our Vision

To create a digitally empowered Telangana where every citizen has easy access to government services, transparent governance, and active participation in local development.

Key Features

Grievance Redressal System

Submit complaints with photo evidence, track status in real-time, and receive timely updates from concerned authorities.

Digital Governance Hub

Access latest announcements, meeting schedules, welfare schemes, and fund usage information from your local authorities.

Transparency & Analytics

View comprehensive analytics on complaint resolution, track progress, and ensure accountability in governance.

How It Works

1
Register & Select Location

Create your account and select your state, district, and village/urban locality.

2
Submit Complaint

Report civic issues with detailed description, category, and photo evidence.

3
Track Progress

Monitor your complaint status and receive updates from concerned authorities.

4
Resolution

Get timely resolution and provide feedback on the quality of service.
